Two years after creating my " Square Peeps " page, my 8-year-old neighbor saw me playing with it and asked if she could try. Of course I said yes. I was curious as to what intrigued her about it, but I was also concerned because she has only used PCs. I did not want her to become discouraged or frustrated by my design because she was using a Mac for the first time.

Not only was she able to figure out a Mac touchpad, she created her own special design through my web page and she was thrilled with her design.







To this day, Olivia still plays with "Your Game" as she calls it. I think we are both amazed by what she creates. From dollhouse layouts to practicing print letters, we are both excited and surprised but what she can design using a grid. She has not once asked me for help which tells me as a designer and developer, the time and effort I put into creating all of the web app features I deemed necessary for human-centered design were essential and this web app has exceeded my expectations.
